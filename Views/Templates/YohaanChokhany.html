About

Yohaan is a 10th Grade Student, studying at Podar International School. He lives at Mumbai, India. He is a national level Indian tennis player. He has won two national championships and has come Runner-up in five more. During the COVID-19 pandemic in Summer 2020, he developed his coding skills. He has created numerous unpublished websites, including a social media messaging website and one website development course to teach his younger sister the delight of website development. He has also delved into the realm of Game Development, recreating retro games like Pong & Legend of Zelda and more contemporary ones like Angry Birds and Pokémon.
